CFP Ideas for Bri
Format: talk | Audience: intermediate
Conference: KubeCon
Track: Observability
==================================================

Idea #1: Rethinking automated testing
Type: angle-based | Topic: automated testing
Abstract: Discover the key concepts behind rethinking automated testing and learn how to apply them in your own projects in the Observability space.

Idea #2: The story of how we navigating team reorgs
Type: experience-based | Topic: navigating team reorgs
Abstract: In this talk, we'll explore the story of how we navigating team reorgs in the Observability space and share practical insights from real-world experience.

Idea #3: How we span and trace logging
Type: experience-based | Topic: span and trace logging
Abstract: Join us for an engaging session on how we span and trace logging. Perfect for intermediate at KubeCon.

Idea #4: Navigating re-orgs in observability teams: Maintaining continuity through change
Type: experience-based | Topic: team reorganization and observability
Abstract: In this talk, we'll explore lessons learned from navigating organizational restructuring in observability teams. Learn practical strategies for maintaining system knowledge, keeping metrics and dashboards relevant, and ensuring monitoring doesn't break when teams shift.

Idea #5: Knowledge transfer done right: How to hand off observability ownership
Type: experience-based | Topic: knowledge transfer and documentation
Abstract: Discover the key concepts behind effective knowledge transfer (KT) in observability contexts. This talk covers best practices for documenting monitoring systems, conducting effective handoffs, and ensuring new team members can confidently own alert response and incident management.
